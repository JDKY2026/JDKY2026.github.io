Hextris: The Addictive Hexagonal Puzzle Game

A fast-paced puzzle game with a hexagonal twist - rotate the hexagon to match colors and prevent overflow.

About Hextris

Hextris is an innovative puzzle game that brings a fresh perspective to the classic block-stacking genre. Instead of traditional grid-based gameplay, Hextris challenges players with a hexagonal playing field where colorful blocks rotate around a central core.

Originally developed as an open-source project, Hextris quickly gained popularity for its simple yet addictive gameplay mechanics, beautiful minimalist design, and increasing difficulty that keeps players coming back for more.

Game Features

  • Unique hexagonal gameplay mechanics
  • Colorful, minimalist design aesthetic
  • Progressive difficulty that tests your reflexes
  • Score tracking system to compete with friends
  • Optimized for both desktop and mobile play

How to Play Hextris

Controls:

  • Left/Right Arrow Keys or A/D: Rotate the hexagon
  • Down Arrow or S: Speed up block descent
  • On mobile: Tap left or right side of the screen to rotate

Objective:

Match three or more blocks of the same color to clear them. Prevent blocks from reaching the outer edge of the playing field. The game gets progressively faster and more challenging as your score increases.

Strategy Tips:

  • Focus on creating color combos to clear multiple blocks at once
  • Watch all sides of the hexagon - danger can come from any direction
  • Plan your moves in advance when possible
  • As the game speeds up, prioritize preventing overflow over making perfect matches
