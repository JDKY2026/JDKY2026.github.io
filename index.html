Monster Survivors

Battle endless waves of monsters and evolve your character to survive the onslaught!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is simple: survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Collect experience points to level up, unlock powerful abilities, and upgrade your character's stats to face the growing challenge.

The longer you survive, the more dangerous the enemies become. Utilize strategic movement and carefully selected upgrades to maximize your survival time and climb the leaderboards!

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Attacks happen automatically when enemies are in range
  • Press Space to use special abilities (when available)
  • Press ESC to pause the game

Tips for Success

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize upgrades that complement your playstyle
  • Collect experience gems quickly before they disappear
  • Focus on area-of-effect abilities when facing large groups
  • Health regeneration upgrades are crucial for long-term survival

Game Features

Endless Action

Face waves of increasingly difficult monsters with no end in sight. How long can you survive?

Character Progression

Level up and choose from various upgrades and abilities to create your unique build.

Dynamic Difficulty

Experience a balanced challenge that scales with your skill and character power level.

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors draws inspiration from popular roguelike and bullet-hell games, offering an accessible yet challenging experience for players of all skill levels. With its simple controls and deep progression system, the game strikes a perfect balance between casual fun and strategic depth.

Each run is unique, with random enemy spawns and upgrade options creating endless possibilities. Whether you have five minutes or an hour to spare, Monster Survivors provides an engaging gaming experience that's hard to put down.
