About Hextris

Game Overview

Hextris is an addictive puzzle game inspired by the classic Tetris but with a hexagonal twist. The game features a hexagon in the center with colored blocks moving toward the center from the outer edges. Your objective is to prevent the blocks from building up and reaching the outer border by rotating the hexagon to match colors.

The game increases in difficulty as you progress, with blocks moving faster and patterns becoming more complex, providing a challenging experience that tests your reflexes and strategic thinking.

How to Play

  • Use the left and right arrow keys to rotate the hexagon
  • Match three or more blocks of the same color to clear them
  • Prevent blocks from building up to the outer edge
  • The game ends when blocks reach the outer boundary
  • Challenge yourself to achieve the highest possible score
